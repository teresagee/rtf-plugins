## How to Add New Ideas

When inspiration strikes:

1. **Pick the right category** (personal stories, current events, audience questions, seasonal, quick hits)
2. **Give it a working title**
3. **Write why it matters** (1-2 sentences)
4. **Add potential angle** (optional - your unique take)
5. **Note the trigger** (optional - what made you think of it)

Don't overthink it. Capture the idea and move on. The ghostwriter will ask questions when you're ready to write.
